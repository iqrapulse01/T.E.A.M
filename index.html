Key Services

1. Scooter Pick-Up, Recharge, and Redeployment

Efficient Fleet Collection:
Our mobile teams, equipped with vans, collect scooters in need of charging or maintenance.
Sustainable Charging Solutions:
Scooters are recharged using renewable energy sources at centralized charging hubs.
Strategic Redeployment:
Fully charged scooters are returned to high-demand areas based on real-time data.

2. Comprehensive Maintenance and Repairs

Routine Maintenance:
Regular checks for brakes, tires, and motor performance ensure safety.
On-Site Repairs:
Address minor issues in the field to reduce downtime.
Advanced Repair Facilities:

3. Fleet Optimization and Analytics

Data-Driven Insights:
Leverage fleet analytics to maximize utilization and operational efficiency.

Predictive Maintenance:

4. Sustainability and Eco-Friendly Solutions

Battery Recycling:
Eco-friendly programs for battery disposal and recycling.

Green Energy: Charging hubs powered by renewable energy sources to reduce carbon footprints.
